How to Write Perfect Prompts?

Writing a prompt is like casting a spell. If you say "Cat", AI gives a cat. But if you say "A fluffy Persian cat sitting on a golden throne, cinematic lighting, 8k resolution", AI creates a masterpiece.

The Formula:

  • Subject: What do you want? (e.g., A Robot)
  • Style: Cyberpunk, Oil Painting, Anime.
  • Lighting: Neon lights, Sunset, Studio lighting.
  • Quality: 4k, 8k, highly detailed, sharp focus.

Try this in our generator: "A futuristic city with flying cars, neon rain, blade runner style, hyper-realistic."
